MyMusic.com was created with the intention to give users a chance to freely discover new music. There exist many music websites in today's world. These platforms provide users with a good music experience in which they can obtain playlists, discover new music, and more. But not everyone is able to pay for the subscription, and then, the experience becomes limited. That is why we want to optimize and enhance the experience of discovering new music for free for people looking to branch out. Current streaming platforms restrict you to genres and pre-made discovery playlists. Our website will use a song or artist the user enjoys to produce new and interesting playlists, with different results every time. This allows for an individualized unique experience per user, unlike the current platforms that make general ones and disperse them to many users.

ENJOY YOUR EXPERIENCE AT MYMUSIC!
